Welcome To Bold Pastel

Jan 1 2022

What is Bold Pastel#

We make web software. We do it because we enjoy making good software. We are unpretentious. By seeking and blundering we learn.

And so when we make creative software, it will not be for want of boldness but because of it.

Attempting to make epic software, one bit at a time, standing on the shoulder of giants.

What do we do?#

We are building micro applications on the web to solve practical problems for creators and professionals. We believe the web is a great equalizer and a community. We strive to learn and share as we go on our journey.

Our current focus is scripting using Go, Rails and Salesforce tools.

How can we help?#

We are always up for a chat on technology and on creative ways to solve vexing problems. We believe in the power of the citizen developer. We truly believe code is just one element of a successful solution. Hit us up if you need us.
